Catholic Youth Organization of Nigeria

Catholic Youth Organization of Nigeria (CYON) is a body in the universl Catholic church. It helps in brimgimg the Catholic youths together under one umbrella. The body enhance the faith and the relationship among the youths of the church through their various activities such as, games, prayers and worship, skill acquisition, excursions and lot more.
